  1. **bcrypt/passlib incompatibility**: passlib 1.7.4 crashes with bcrypt 4.x.
     Replaced passlib with direct bcrypt usage in security.py.
  2. **JWT sub claim type**: python-jose requires `sub` to be a string, not int.
